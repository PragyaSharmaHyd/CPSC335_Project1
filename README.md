# CPSC335_Project1
Project 1 Algorithm 1 for CPSC 335 
By: Pragya Sharma, April Perez, Raphael Padua, Marcos Garcia

The following algorithm is 1 out of the 2 algorithms for our CPSC 335 project. In this algorith, we are aiming to form a seating chart with pairs of people based on their value. For example, we would like (0,1), (2,3), (4,5), (6,7), and so on. 

The goal of this algorithm is to take an array of numbers (must be even and less than or equal to 30) and arrange them in the correct seating order.
